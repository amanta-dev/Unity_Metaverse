2. Product Description

EnglishForest is an educational application for children that teaches English through a 3D explorable world and mini-games. It is delivered entirely as software through the Apple App Store and Google Play. There is no physical item, electrical component, battery, or chemical substance associated with the product.

3. Safety Markings

As a digital application, the App does not carry physical compliance markings (such as CE marking) because no physical product safety directives apply. No physical warning labels are required or provided.

4. Age Suitability

The App is rated 4+ and is designed to be suitable for young children. It contains no content that is unsuitable for the rated age group.

5. Safe Use Guidance

  • Use the App on a device that is in good working condition and follow the device manufacturer’s safety guidance.
  • For children, we recommend supervised use and reasonable limits on screen time.
  • Subscriptions and other purchases are protected by an in-app parental gate and by the store’s native purchase-authentication settings.
