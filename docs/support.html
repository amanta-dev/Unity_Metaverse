Subscriptions

  • EnglishForest offers auto-renewable subscriptions (monthly and yearly) that unlock all premium mini-game themes.
  • Manage or cancel your subscription anytime in your device’s store account settings (App Store or Google Play). Deleting the app does not cancel a subscription.
  • Restore purchases: open the subscription panel in the app and tap “Restore Purchases” (useful after reinstalling or changing devices).

For Parents

  • Purchases and external links are protected by an in-app parental gate.
  • The app contains no third-party ads and no third-party analytics.
  • You can also enable purchase authentication in your device’s store settings.

Privacy & Data

  • EnglishForest stores progress locally on your device. To delete all data, simply uninstall the app.
  • See our Privacy Policy and Terms of Use.
